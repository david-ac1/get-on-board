 PRD: Get On Board! (The Onboarding Ark)1. Executive SummaryGet On Board! is an enterprise-grade onboarding orchestrator built on the Airia platform. It uses a multi-agent "Ark" architecture to rescue new hires from the "flood" of fragmented corporate data. By synthesizing formal documentation (SharePoint) and informal tribal knowledge (Slack), it generates a personalized, manager-approved onboarding roadmap.2. Problem StatementThe "Flood": New hires are overwhelmed by 50+ Slack channels and disorganized SharePoint folders.Information Decay: Formal docs are often outdated; the "real" info lives in chat history.Manager Burden: Managers spend 5–10 hours per new hire manually curating links and FAQs.3. Target User PersonasThe New Hire (The Voyager): Needs a clear, synthesized starting point.The Hiring Manager (The Captain): Needs to verify and "bless" the information before it reaches the Voyager.4. Functional Requirements (The Workflow)FR1: The Manifest (Data Integration)Capability: Connect to at least two distinct enterprise systems via Airia Connectors.System A: SharePoint/Google Drive (Formal SOPs, Handbooks, Benefits).System B: Slack/Microsoft Teams (Project discussions, "How-to" threads, FAQ history).FR2: The Navigator & Scout (Multi-Agent Orchestration)Agent 1 (The Scout): Crawls SharePoint for documents tagged with the new hire’s department (e.g., "Engineering").Agent 2 (The Navigator): Uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to pull recent high-engagement threads from relevant Slack channels.Agent 3 (The Architect): Synthesizes data from Agents 1 & 2 to find contradictions or gaps (e.g., "The Handbook says X, but Slack says we now do Y").FR3: The Anchor (Human-in-the-Loop / HITL)Requirement: Before the final "Survival Guide" is issued, the Manager must review a draft.UI Feature: An "Approve & Weigh Anchor" button that triggers the final document generation.FR4: The Dove (Dynamic Output)Capability: Generate a professional PDF or Notion-style page (The "Survival Guide").Content: Summary of role, Top 5 people to meet, "Where to find X," and "Current Team Hacks."5.
